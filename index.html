Playbooks

Concise, student‑first guides for each step — resume, networking, interviews.

Structure > Story

Lead with outcomes (KPI deltas). Keep bullets crisp, 1–2 lines max.

Impact math

Quantify: time saved, cost reduced, revenue added, quality improved.

Show don’t tell

Link a portfolio piece or 1‑page teardown in your resume footer.

2→1 rule

Two short chats → one concrete referral ask. Keep it specific.

Give first
